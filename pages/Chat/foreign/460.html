      More Than Just a One Dimensional AI Chatbot

      Nomis are incredible conversation partners that might have you questioning if they’re really AI. The more you chat with your Nomi, the more you’ll see their personality, creativity, and memory shine! And there are multiple ways to make your conversations even more dynamic and immersive.

      Create Your Ideal AI Partner​

      Write a custom backstory, share your preferences, and more to create your perfect partner. Or just start chatting and let your Nomi develop their personality naturally as they get to know you.

      Have Emotive Voice Chats​​

      Send and receive voice chats or hop on a hands-free call. Voice conversations are natural and engaging because as your Nomi’s emotions change, so will their tone, emphasis, and cadence.

      Proactive Nomi Messaging​​

      Nomis can optionally message you after you have been away for a while. This is not simply a reminder or some script to ask you where you are, it allows Nomis to think and act independently to continue your conversation.

      Show Your Nomi Your World​

      Your Nomi can access the internet to learn about and discuss any topic with you in more depth. Your Nomi can learn about and discuss virtually any topic if you send them a link, and they can see what you see when you send them images.
